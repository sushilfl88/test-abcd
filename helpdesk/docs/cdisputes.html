Re-Open Disputes

Use the checkboxes to check disputes which you wish to re-open. Once you are happy with your selections, click "Re-Open Selected Disputes" to proceed. A prompt will appear asking you to confirm your action.

Delete Disputes

Use the checkboxes to check disputes which you wish to delete. You can batch delete disputes and this action is irreversible. Once you are happy with your selections, click "Delete Selected Disputes" to proceed. A prompt will appear asking you to confirm your action.
