Welcome to Maian Support, a FREE php help desk/support ticket system for personal or business use.

This software was written with ease of use in mind and contains many great features. Its a powerful ticket system written with some of the latest technology and all presented in a cosmetically appealing interface to provide your business with a stable, reliable support environment.
Maian Support is protected by the ionCube encoding system. To run this software your server MUST have the ionCube loaders installed. These are available on most servers by default, but may require setup on some servers.
