Overview

The visitor portal is where a visitor logs into the system to view his/her tickets. The main screen displays some ticket stats and latest tickets and can always be accessed via the "My Account" link on the top menu bar.

Sign In

Visitor signs in via the "Sign In" link in the left menu.

Sign In Page

Email and password are required to sign in. A "Forgot Password" link is also shown for anyone who may have forgot their password.
